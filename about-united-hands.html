About United Hands

WHO WE ARE: We are a multipurpose co-operative society established with the aim of bringing together and coordinating likeminded people in order to encourage them to save their finances, invest wisely and organize a pool of private funds which will serve as a reliable source of credit and investment opportunities for members. UNITED HANDS MULTIPURPOSE CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETY is licensed by the Abuja Municipal Area Council (AMAC) F.C.T under the Cooperative Societies Act 2013 as Amended.

WHAT WE DO: We understand that many individuals are making concerted efforts towards personal financial growth and economic development despite the harsh economic climate in the country. Our mission is to ease the burden of funding by encouraging people to save and enjoy the benefits of crowdfunding by collaborating with likeminded individuals. Our philosophy is that there is amazing power and greater possibilities in collaboration. Through our services, people can achieve their financial dreams with or without the exorbitant traditional bank loans.
